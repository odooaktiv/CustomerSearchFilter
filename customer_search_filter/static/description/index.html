Customer Search Filter

Aktiv Software has prepared the "Customer Search Filter" module with the aim of finding a customer as per its mobile and phone number, city, email and its job position in any object.

Below mentioned are the features of this module:

  • We can find a customer from its mobile and phone number in any object.
  • We can also find a customer from its city name, email and its job position.

Prerequisites before Installation

There are no prerequisites required before the installation.

Configurations

There are no Configuration required after the installation.

  • First we'll see that the customer has been filtered according to its phone number in sale.order model or any other model.
  • Now we'll see that the customer has been filtered according to its mobile number if it is available.
  • Below we would see that the customer has been filtered according to its city.
  • In the below screen we will see that the customer is filtered according to its email.
  • In the below screen we will see that the customer is filtered according to its job position.
